Proof of Presence

Like FOAM's Presence Claims but for commercial spaces. Multiple signals produce a confidence-scored, tamper-evident presence record:

{
  "space_uri": "pg://workshop17/cpt/silo/floor-2/room-204",
  "tenant": "tenant-9f8e7d",
  "entered": "2026-04-04T09:15:00+02:00",
  "exited":  "2026-04-04T11:32:00+02:00",
  "signals": [
    { "source": "salto",  "event": "access_granted", "at": "09:15:00" },
    { "source": "sensor", "event": "occupied",        "at": "09:15:12" },
    { "source": "sensor", "event": "vacant",           "at": "11:31:48" },
    { "source": "salto",  "event": "access_granted", "at": "11:32:00" }
  ],
  "confidence": 0.98,
  "hash": "sha256:e3b0c44..."
}

Used for: billing verification, dispute resolution, occupancy analytics, fire safety compliance. Written to the existing Event Ledger — Proximity Green's immutable audit trail.

Partner Skills Hub

Proximity Green runs their own branded skills hub at their domain, powered by the 2nth.ai skill tree. Their hub shows only the skills relevant to shared-space operations while inheriting the full platform infrastructure.

How it works

skills.proximity.green          (partner-branded hub)
        |
        ├── biz/shared-space-ops    (Proximity Green skills)
        ├── biz/tenant-billing
        ├── biz/access-governance
        |
        ├── biz/hubspot-crm         (shared 2nth.ai skills)
        ├── fin/cash-flow-forecaster
        ├── collab/meeting-agent
        |
        └── 2nth.ai skill tree      (full resolution via API)
            └── skills.2nth.ai/resolve?path=biz/shared-space-ops

The partner hub cherry-picks skills from the 2nth.ai tree relevant to their domain. Skills resolve dependencies automatically — if shared-space-ops depends on hubspot-crm, both are included.

Setting up a partner skills hub

  1. Fork the skills repo Fork 2nth-ai/skills into your org. Add your domain-specific skills.
  2. Create your hub Use the 2nth-ai/clients template to scaffold a branded skills hub. Configure your domain, logo, and which skill categories to display.
  3. Map your domains Choose which 2nth.ai skill domains appear on your hub. Proximity Green maps: biz/* (all business skills), fin/* (finance), collab/* (collaboration).
  4. Deploy to your domain Deploy to Cloudflare Pages on your own domain (skills.proximity.green). The hub connects to agents.2nth.ai for skill resolution and AI inference.
  5. Token billing flows through Your customers use tokens from the 2nth.ai platform. When they execute a Proximity Green skill, your 15% revenue share is tracked automatically.

Architecture

Customer visits skills.proximity.green
    → Browses shared-space skills (branded UI)

    → Runs a skill (e.g. "Analyse my occupancy data")

Key architectural decisions

  • No blockchain required. FOAM's decentralised verification is replaced by operator-verified IoT signals. The Event Ledger (Cloudflare D1) provides the immutable audit trail.
  • Space URIs replace geohashes. Hierarchical, human-readable, and stable across renames. Shorter URIs aggregate; longer URIs resolve to specific spaces.
  • Confidence scoring replaces consensus. Multiple signal sources (access + sensor + booking) produce a 0-1 confidence score instead of Byzantine fault-tolerant agreement.
  • Gallery tiers replace token staking. Operators progress through Listed → Verified → Certified based on data quality and operational track record, not token economics.
  • Gallery is a distribution channel. Tenants discover spaces through the gallery. Operators pay tokens for premium listing. Revenue share flows through the 2nth.ai skill tree.
