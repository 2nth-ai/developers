fin/payments-infrastructure

Payments Infrastructure

fin/smart-cash

SMART Cash systems for emerging markets: intelligent cash management, demand-driven ATM replenishment, cash cycle analytics, CIT route optimisation, and the business model innovations needed to keep cash accessible as transaction volumes shift to digital. The South African playbook for cash infrastructure sustainability.

Cash systems Payments

fin/atm-economics

ATM network viability: interchange economics, deployment strategy, white-label vs. bank-owned models, cash recycler adoption, uptime optimisation, and regulatory requirements. How to maintain accessible cash infrastructure in a market where digital payments are growing but cash dependency remains high.

ATM Infrastructure

Cash as a financial inclusion tool: unbanked and underbanked populations, cash-to-digital bridges, agent banking, mobile money integration, and the role of physical currency in reaching vulnerable communities. South African context: social grants (SASSA), informal economy, rural access.

Inclusion Cash

Central Bank Digital Currency implications for the cash ecosystem: Project Khokha (SARB), wholesale vs. retail CBDC models, coexistence with physical cash, privacy considerations, and the impact on commercial bank deposits, payment service providers, and cash infrastructure operators.
