The role: finding humans for AI-powered work

AI doesn't replace people — it changes what people do. Every 2nth.ai partner deployment creates demand for humans with new skills:

Warehouse & Logistics

ScanMan + DroneScan need pickers who work with AI pick-path optimisers, warehouse managers who read AI dashboards, and technicians who maintain scanning robots.

Hospitality & Shared Spaces

Proximity Green operators need space managers who use AI occupancy tools, billing administrators who work with automated invoicing, and facilities staff trained on IoT systems.

Conservation & Agriculture

Luthuli lodges need lodge managers fluent in off-grid tech. Soleil Sitrus needs packhouse supervisors who interpret AI yield forecasts. AI augments — humans execute.

Professional & Advisory

Partners like Ctrl Future and Gananda need analysts who use AI research tools, consultants who leverage AI strategy skills, and administrators who manage token-based billing.

Services
