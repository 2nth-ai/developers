Real-world use cases

🏢 Workshop17 — 16 locations, occupancy optimisation

Workshop17 uses the Shared Space Operations skill to forecast occupancy across all 16 sites. When Watershed hits 92%, dynamic pricing kicks in automatically. When Newlands drops below 55%, the system triggers a targeted campaign for nearby members.

Skills: shared-space-ops, tenant-billing, dynamics-365, xero-accounting, cash-flow-forecaster

🏛 KOFISI Kigali — tenant dispute resolution

A tenant queries a billing charge. The Tenant Billing skill pulls the full audit trail from the event ledger — access logs, contract terms, rate applied — and drafts a resolution within seconds. Credit note issued, billing rule updated, no manual investigation.

Skills: tenant-billing, access-governance, compliance-checker

🔐 Corporate campus — after-hours access alert

A flex member badges in at 10:47pm on a Saturday. Access Governance denies entry (policy: weekday 8-6 only), alerts security, and surfaces upgrade options for the tenant admin: Enterprise plan or temporary after-hours pass.

Skills: access-governance, tenant-billing, shared-space-ops

🏫 Student accommodation — compliance check

Ahead of a municipal inspection, the Compliance Checker scans all facilities against OHS regulations, fire safety codes, and POPIA requirements. Generates a readiness report with flagged items and recommended actions — 3 days before the inspector arrives.

Skills: compliance-checker, shared-space-ops, meeting-agent
