AI skills

Five skills that change the game

Each skill runs on the 2nth.ai platform. Ask questions in plain language. Get answers from your warehouse data.

Building now

Inventory Accuracy Intelligence

Detects stock count drift in real-time. Identifies systematic errors by zone, shift, or product category. Schedules smart cycle counts where they matter most.

"Which zones have the highest variance this month?"
Building now

Shelf Life & Obsolescence Manager

Predicts which stock expires before sale. Recommends markdown timing, flags FIFO violations, generates waste reduction reports. Saves money before product hits the bin.

"What's at risk of expiring in the next 14 days?"
Q3 2026

Pick Path Optimiser

Analyses picking patterns and warehouse layout. Reduces travel time per order, suggests bin relocation for fast-moving SKUs, balances workload across pickers.

"Rearrange Zone B for this week's top 50 SKUs."
Q3 2026

Receiving Anomaly Detection

Catches discrepancies at goods-in: quantity vs PO, unexpected SKUs, damaged barcodes. Builds supplier reliability scores from receiving history.

"Which suppliers have the most short-ships this quarter?"
Q4 2026

Warehouse Operations Dashboard

Real-time warehouse health: throughput, error rates, labour productivity, space utilisation, dock-to-stock time. Conversational AI — ask anything about your operations.

"Give me a morning briefing for all three warehouses."
