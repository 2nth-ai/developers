How to find your ski

  1. Wait for the app to find your SkiSpot devices
  2. Select the device for the ski you want to find
  3. Hold the phone horizontal and close to your chest
  4. Press the green START button
  5. Rotate your body 360 degrees slowly until the arrow points forward again
  6. Press the red STOP button
  7. The arrow turns blue and points to your ski
  8. Walk in that direction — watch the RSSI graph. Up = closer!
